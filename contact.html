Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your response times?

We typically respond to emails within 2-4 hours during business hours. Phone calls and WhatsApp messages are answered immediately when available. Urgent matters receive priority attention 24/7.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary initial consultations for all our services. This allows us to understand your needs and provide you with the best possible solution without any obligation.

What languages do you support?

Our team is fluent in Portuguese, English, Spanish, and French. We can accommodate clients in these languages for all our services including consultancy, legal matters, and business communications.

Can you help with urgent business matters?

Absolutely! We understand that business needs can be time-sensitive. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +351 920 317 545 or message us on WhatsApp for immediate assistance, even outside regular business hours.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we serve clients worldwide. Our strategic location in Lisbon makes us the perfect partner for businesses looking to expand to Portugal or use Portugal as a gateway to European and international markets.
